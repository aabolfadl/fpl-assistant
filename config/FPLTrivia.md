# FPL Trivia: Likely Questions for an AI Assistant

## Player Performance & Statistics

- How many goals did [player] score in [season]?
- How many assists did [player] make in [season]?
- How many clean sheets did [player] keep in [season]?
- How many total points did [player] score in [season]?
- What is the average points per game for [player]?
- What is the highest points [player] scored in a single match?
- What is the lowest points [player] scored in a single match?
- How many matches did [player] play in [season]?
- What is [player]'s minutes per point ratio?
- What is [player]'s best gameweek performance?
- What is [player]'s worst gameweek performance?
- What is [player]'s form trend over the season?
- What is [player]'s ICT (Influence, Creativity, Threat) breakdown?

## Player vs Team & Opponent Analysis

- How many points did [player] score against [team]?
- Which team did [player] score the most points against?
- Which team did [player] score the fewest points against?
- What is [player]'s home vs away points split?

## Top Performers & Recommendations

- Who are the top scoring players in [position]?
- Who are the top players by [stat] (goals, assists, clean sheets, etc.)?
- Who offers the best value for money?

## Team Analysis & Fixtures

- What is the average goals scored per game for [team]?
- What is the average goals conceded per game for [team]?
- How many clean sheets did [team] keep?
- Who is the most impactful player on [team]?

## Miscellaneous & Trivia

- Which player had the most yellow/red cards?
- Which player had the most goal contributions (goals + assists)?
- Which player played the most minutes?

---

Feel free to ask about any player, team, stat, or FPL scenario!
